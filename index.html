Brief Biography

I am a master's student majoring in pattern recognition and intelligent systems at the University of Science and Technology of China(USTC）.

I graduated from Wuhan University of Technology (WHUT) School of Automotive Engineering in 2021. I majored in energy and power engineering and received a bachelor's degree in engineering from Wuhan University of Technology. When I first entered the school, my college was the School of Transportation. In my sophomore year, I transferred my major to the School of Automotive Engineering, and I have been serving as a class cadre. I was ranked 3/141 during my undergraduate period. I was successfully excluded from the University of Science and Technology of China for further studies. My current research direction is driverless cars.
